## The Problem We Solve

Organizations of every size struggle with the same challenge: **they don't know where their assets are, who has them, or whether they're being used.**

The typical result:
- Equipment is purchased twice because no one can find the first one
- Warranties expire silently, voiding repair coverage
- Maintenance issues go unreported until a breakdown causes downtime
- Audits take days instead of hours
- Liability grows when high-value assets can't be accounted for

Manual methods — spreadsheets, whiteboards, shared folders — fail at scale. They're always outdated, prone to errors, and invisible to the people who need the information most.

---

## Our Solution

**AssetHub** is a cloud-based asset management platform that gives organizations a **single, real-time view** of every asset they own — from computers and vehicles to tools and furniture.

AssetHub makes it easy to:

| What | How |
|------|-----|
| Track assets | QR code scanning + searchable digital registry |
| Control access | Role-based permissions (Admin, Manager, Member) |
| Manage maintenance | Built-in ticket system with priority and status tracking |
| Monitor warranties | Dashboard alerts for assets expiring within 30 days |
| Audit usage | Complete, tamper-evident activity log |
| Scale across teams | Multi-tenant architecture — one platform, multiple departments or companies |

## Business Impact

### Cost savings

- **Eliminate "ghost assets"** — assets listed on the books but actually lost or decommissioned
- **Prevent duplicate purchases** — instant lookup tells you if you already own what you need
- **Catch warranties before they lapse** — avoid paying out-of-pocket for covered repairs

### Operational efficiency

- **Reduce equipment search time from hours to seconds** with QR scanning
- **Maintenance response time drops** when issues are reported digitally with full context
- **Onboarding is faster** when new employees can self-serve asset requests

### Compliance & accountability

- **Full audit trail** of every action — who checked out what, when, and for how long
- **Tenant data isolation** ensures each department or client's data stays private
- **Role-based access** means staff see only what they should

## Key Metrics to Watch

Once deployed, AssetHub tracks and surfaces:

- **Asset utilization rate** — what % of assets are actively in use
- **Mean time to repair** — average days from ticket open to resolution
- **Checkout frequency** — most and least used equipment
- **Warranty coverage** — % of assets with valid warranty
- **Asset by location** — distribution across facilities
